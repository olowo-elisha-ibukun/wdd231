WDD 231 – W05 Project: Site Plan

Site Name

Ondo State Chamber of Commerce

This name was chosen because it represents an organization dedicated to supporting businesses and economic development in Ondo State, Nigeria.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote local businesses in Ondo State by providing a business directory, sharing chamber news and events, and offering information about membership and services.

Scenarios

  • How can a business owner in Ondo State register their business with the Chamber of Commerce?
  • Where can visitors find a list of registered businesses in Ondo State?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Dark Navy Blue (#0b132b): Used for the header, buttons, and major headings to create a professional and trustworthy appearance.

Secondary Color: Deep Blue (#1c2541): Used for the navigation menu and footer backgrounds.

Accent Color: Teal (#5bc0be): Used for icons, highlights, borders, and hover effects to add visual interest.

Neutral Colors: Light Gray (#f9f9f9) and White (#ffffff): Used for page backgrounds and content sections to improve readability.

Typography

Roboto is used as the primary font for the website. Headings use a bold weight to create hierarchy and emphasis, while body text uses a regular weight to ensure clarity and readability across all sections, including navigation, content cards, and modal windows.
